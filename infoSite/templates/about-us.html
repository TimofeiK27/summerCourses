About Us

Evan Meltzer

Evan loved filmmaking since he was 8 years old. He has been using Premiere Pro for 7 years and Blender for 2 years. Evan has experience filming and editing promotional, educational, and informational content for a variety of organizations including Lucky Pen, 5th Avenue Theater, ASSA Games, Eastside Prep, iMiracle, and more.
